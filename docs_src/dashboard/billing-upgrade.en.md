# Upgrade & billing

The dashboard includes a screen to purchase or manage the **Dashboard** plan.

## Upgrade


- On **Free**: you can **Buy Pro** or **Buy Business**.
- On **Pro/Business**: you can open the **Stripe billing portal** to manage the subscription.

All subscriptions are managed by Stripe portal.

You can change between subscriptions or cancel when you want.
